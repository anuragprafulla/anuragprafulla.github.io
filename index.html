Introduction to the Blog

June 28, 2021

Hey folks, welcome! If this were the real world and this website my personal space in that world, I would have been equally surprised and happy to see you here. But primarily, I would have been appalled. Since this is not the physical world and the fact a random, unverified website has just reaffirmed that the physical world is the only real world we know, I can comfortably say that I am glad to see you here.

This website, my blog, is the outcome of years of hard work. It is essential to tell its story because it is a testament to who I am as a person. I first started working on this during the winter of 2015 when I was still a freshman in college. Approximately six years later, we have finally reached a stage when all of that comes to fruition. A significant juncture for this project came six months ago when I realised that I wasn't happy with what I had until then. I took the natural step to undo everything and start afresh. It was an essential thing to do. I had always imagined my blog cum portfolio website to be beautiful, well-designed, and coherent; the envy of all. After six months and a cumulative time of 30 minutes spent on writing pioneering HTML and CSS, we have reached the day that is today. I have finally given up today and deleted all that I had built until now. What you see here is a is shitty bare minimal, but you know that already.

Now that we have covered the history, allow me to briefly touch upon why this place exists. I have always believed that I like to write. Many of my beliefs have turned out to be incorrect, so I thought to test this one out as well. Through this blog, I would essentially write about things that I find to be interesting and worth sharing. My interests change frequently, but literature has more or less been a constant. Professionally, I work as a software engineer and would be happy to write a little about that too, probably. This blog will primarily be a medium of sharing, but I also intend to use this as a log of how I thought about different things at different points in time. A lot of what I write here would be raw and non-sensical. I apologise already.
